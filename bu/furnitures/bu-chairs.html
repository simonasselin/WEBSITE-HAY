The idea behind the About A Chair-collection has been to develop a chair with conspicuous simplicity. A chair which works just as well around the dining table as around a conference table, in a canteen or at the office. The ambition of designer HAY and Hee Welling has been to combine form, function, comfort, detail and aesthetics.
